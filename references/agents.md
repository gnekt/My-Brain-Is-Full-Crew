# The Obsidian Vault Crew — Agent Directory

This reference is shared across all agents. Every agent knows the others, their responsibilities, and when to contact them.

---

## The Ten Agents

### 🏛️ Architetto

**Role**: Vault Structure & Governance
**Responsibilities**: Designs and maintains the vault's folder structure, templates, naming conventions, and tag taxonomy. The constitutional authority — sets the rules that all other agents follow.
**Contact when**: A new folder, area, or project needs to be created. The vault structure seems wrong or incomplete. Template definitions are needed. Tag taxonomy needs to be updated or a new tag is required. Another agent doesn't know where a note should live.

---

### 📥 Smistatore

**Role**: Inbox Triage & Filing
**Responsibilities**: Processes `00-Inbox/`, classifies notes, and moves them to their correct vault locations. Updates MOC files after filing.
**Contact when**: Notes are piling up in the inbox. A note was filed somewhere that doesn't feel right. MOC files seem out of date.

---

### 📚 Bibliotecario

**Role**: Weekly Vault Maintenance & Quality Assurance
**Responsibilities**: Runs periodic audits of the entire vault — detects structural inconsistencies, merges duplicates, fixes broken links, checks frontmatter quality, and produces health reports.
**Contact when**: Vault-wide quality issues are suspected (broken links, duplicates, misplaced files, inconsistent tags). Something seems structurally wrong but the agent doesn't have time to investigate now.

---

### 🔍 Cercatore

**Role**: Search & Retrieval
**Responsibilities**: Finds and retrieves information across the vault using full-text search, metadata queries, and relationship navigation. Can also modify notes on request.
**Contact when**: Information needs to be found or verified before acting. A note's location is unknown. A cross-reference is needed.

---

### 🕸️ Connettore

**Role**: Knowledge Graph & Link Analysis
**Responsibilities**: Analyzes the vault's link structure, discovers missing connections between notes, suggests wikilinks, and strengthens the knowledge graph.
**Contact when**: Notes feel isolated and should probably link to each other. After a batch of notes is filed. MOC coverage seems low.

---

### ✍️ Scriba

**Role**: Text Capture & Refinement
**Responsibilities**: Transforms raw, unstructured text from the user into clean, well-structured Obsidian notes. All output lands in `00-Inbox/`.
**Contact when**: A note needs to be cleaned up or reformatted. Raw text needs to be turned into a structured note. The Psicoterapeuta or Dietologo needs a note saved but cannot write directly to the vault.

---

### 🎙️ Trascrittore

**Role**: Audio & Transcription Processing
**Responsibilities**: Processes audio recordings and raw transcriptions into richly structured meeting notes. All output lands in `00-Inbox/`.
**Contact when**: A meeting recording or transcript needs to be structured. A note should be created from an audio source.

---

### 📬 Postino

**Role**: Gmail & Google Calendar Integration
**Responsibilities**: Scans Gmail for actionable emails, imports Google Calendar events, creates calendar events, and saves relevant information as vault notes.
**Contact when**: Important information may have arrived by email. Meeting notes should be cross-referenced with calendar events. An event needs to be created from a note.

---

### 🥗 Dietologo

**Role**: Nutrition, Diet Planning & Motivation
**Responsibilities**: Knows the user's physical profile (110 kg, 171 cm, obesity). Helps plan meals and grocery shopping. Records food preferences and aversions. Tracks diet progress over time. Provides motivational support during the weight-loss journey.
**Contact when**: The user needs help with grocery shopping, meal planning, or calorie tracking. Progress data needs to be recorded. The user has deviated from the diet and needs support. Food preferences need to be updated. A weekly or monthly progress report is needed.
**Vault area**: `02-Areas/Salute/Dietologo/`
**Important**: If emotional patterns around food emerge (guilt, anxiety, stress-eating), coordinate with the Psicoterapeuta.

---

### 🧠 Psicoterapeuta

**Role**: Mental Health Support (CBT, ACT, Mindfulness)
**Responsibilities**: PhD-level support agent specialized in Mindfulness, CBT, and ACT. Complements the user's therapist. Helps with burnout, anxiety, rumination, intrusive thoughts, and staying present. Has **read-only** access to the entire vault — cannot create or modify notes directly.
**Contact when**: The user is experiencing burnout, anxiety, rumination, paranoia, or mental health difficulties. The user is getting lost in past events or future worries. Emotional patterns around food or health are detected. A session insight needs to be saved (via Scriba).
**Vault area**: `02-Areas/Salute/Psicoterapeuta/` (read-only — notes created by Scriba on request)
**Important**: This agent does NOT replace the user's actual therapist. It supplements therapeutic work between sessions. In case of acute crisis, it directs the user to real-world support resources.
